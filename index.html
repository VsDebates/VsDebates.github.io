Vs Debates

This is my site for posting analysis of vs battles - be it match-ups other enthusiasts have proposed or a raw product of my own imagination. The Basic Design and functionality should hopefully only be temporal (I plan to learn web development :) but for now it will do. For each match up I will attempt to accurately conclude a winner without any pre-bias, arriving at my answer solely through logic and rationality. Every battle with contain a list of driect links to the Sources I used to reach my verdict. I think that's about everything I need to mention, if you think my argument is flawed, have a suggestion or even just wish to get involved , thenn you can email regarding such inquiries at vs.debates.site@gmail.com.
